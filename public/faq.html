Q:
How does refinancing my vehicle work?
A:
All we need is a little information from you to get started. Then our team takes care of the rest:
Step 1
Provide your personal, vehicle and employment information.
Step 2
Our loan consultants will determine which loans you qualify for and walk you through your options.
Step 3
We’ll submit your application to your chosen lender.
Step 4
Auto Approve will generate all of your new loan and title documents. We’ll send them to you for an e-signature.
Step 5
Auto Approve will handle the rest and get your loan funded. We’ll also work with the DMV to take care of the vehicle title.
Step 6
You’ll begin to make your payments to the new lender.
It’s that simple and fast!
